Food Tour - Murals of the Mission

10 reviews
Previous Next
About Menu Details Reviews

Accompany Chef James on a farmers' market tour, then prepare and enjoy a 4-course lunch in his home.

Are you curious to learn about how a chef goes about choosing their ingredients? This is a great opportunity to shop with Chef James at the Ferry Plaza Farmers' Market, plan your meal, and ultimately prepare a four-course lunch back at his home. The price includes all ingredients for the meal, an interactive cooking class, and a seated lunch.

The price includes all ingredients for the meal, an interactive cooking class, and a seated lunch.

Chef James welcomes guests to bring wine, and will be happy to make recommendations for pairings.

Menu

Market Tour Menu

Menu will be based on what guests enjoy as well as what is seasonal at the market, including vegetables, proteins, and fruits. Additional items will be sourced from local shops.
