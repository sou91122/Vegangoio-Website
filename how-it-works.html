What is Vegangoio

Vegangoio offers the best in plant-based culinary experiences by pairing people and companies with some of the world’s top plant-based chefs for vegan cooking classes and private eventsand culinary team bonding events

Who are the plant-based chefs

Vegangoio chefs are some of the best plant-based chefs in the world and come from an array of culinary backgrounds. From classically trained to professional food caterers, all of our chefs are hand-selected to meet our highest quality standards.

Where are the venues?

ii. Vegan experiences are hosted at the chef's place, your place, or your workplace. Plant-based chef’s locations are all pre-approved and range from premium venues to event spaces to private homes. Alternatively, have the chef come to you by selecting "My Place" when booking.
